About Me

The freedom and excitement of creativity and my love for puzzles and logic often seemed disparate in the professional avenues I investigated. This is truly my calling, and overcoming the hurdles to attending Georgia Tech Full Stack Coding Cohort is just the first step into a career that I can not wait to delve into.
